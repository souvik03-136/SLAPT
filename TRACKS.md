# 🎵 Community Tracks

A growing collection of beats, loops, and full arrangements made with SLAPT.

**Want to share yours?** See [How to Submit](#how-to-submit) below.  
**Just want to show off?** Drop it in [GitHub Discussions → Show & Tell](https://github.com/souvik03-136/slapt/discussions) — no PR needed.

---

## How to Submit

### Option A — Quick Share (no PR needed)
Post in [Discussions → Show & Tell](https://github.com/souvik03-136/slapt/discussions). Paste your code, describe the vibe, done.

### Option B — Add to This Repo (permanent)

1. Fork the repo
2. Create a new file at: `tracks/your-github-username--track-name.md`
3. Copy the template from `tracks/TEMPLATE.md`
4. Open a PR with title: `add(tracks): your track name`

### 🌙 Midnight Study Session
**By:** [@souvik03-136](https://github.com/souvik03-136) · **Genre:** lofi-hiphop · **BPM:** 72 · **Key:** Am  
**Mood:** late night / focused / rainy  
**Tags:** `lofi` `rhodes` `vinyl` `rain`

> Classic lo-fi structure — dusty drums, jazzy chords, walking bass, rain in the background.

```
@genre lofi-hiphop
@tempo 72 bpm
@key Am

atmosphere:
  vinyl crackle at 20% volume
  rain sounds softly in background

drums with swing(60%):
  kick pattern [1, 2.75, 3]
  snare on 2 and 4
  snare velocity random(0.7 to 0.9)
  hihat closed 8 times
  apply bitcrush(10bit)
  compress heavily

chords using rhodes piano:
  progression Am7 -> Fmaj7 -> Dm7 -> E7
  voicing spread
  rhythm whole notes with slight anticipation
  reverb(medium, dreamy)
  tremolo(gentle, 4Hz)

bass walking the roots:
  follow chord progression
  sound mellow
  filter warm

section intro:
  only drums and atmosphere
  fade in over 4 bars

section verse:
  add chords after 4 bars
  add bass after 4 bars

section outro:
  fade out everything over 8 bars
  keep vinyl crackle till end

make it dusty
```

### 🥁 Boom Bap Basics
**By:** [@souvik03-136](https://github.com/souvik03-136) · **Genre:** boom-bap · **BPM:** 90 · **Key:** Dm  
**Mood:** gritty / energetic / classic  
**Tags:** `boom-bap` `rhodes` `walking-bass`

> Hard-hitting boom bap. Wide velocity range on snare for that human feel.

```
@genre boom-bap
@tempo 90 bpm
@key Dm

drums:
  kick on 1 and 3
  snare on 2 and 4
  snare velocity random(0.6 to 1.0)
  hihat closed 8 times
  hihat open occasionally

chords using rhodes piano:
  progression Dm7 -> Cmaj7 -> Am7 -> Gmaj7
  voicing spread

bass walking the roots:
  follow chord progression

bring energy up
```
